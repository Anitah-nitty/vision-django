Payment Frequently Asked Questions

- Is my card information secure?
Yes. Vision Electronics uses industry-standard 256-bit SSL encryption. We never store your card details on our servers; all transactions are processed through Stripe, a world leader in payment security.
- Do you support local payment methods like M-Pesa?
Currently, this portal handles International Card payments. If you wish to pay via M-Pesa, please select the "Mobile Money" option in the delivery step or contact our customer care for a direct payment link.
- Why was my card declined?
Card declines can happen due to incorrect CVV, expired dates, or daily spending limits. Please ensure your card is enabled for international online transactions and try again.
- When will my order be processed after payment?
Once the payment is successful, your order status will change to "Processed" immediately. You will receive an automated email confirmation with your order receipt.
- I was charged but saw an error page. What do I do?
Please do not attempt to pay twice. Check your email for a confirmation first. If no email arrived within 10 minutes, contact our support team at support@visionelectronics.co.ke with your transaction ID.
