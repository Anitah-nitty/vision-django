Tech Support & FAQs

Everything you need to know about your gadgets, orders, and warranty.

1. Are all electronics sold here original and brand new?

Yes, we guarantee that all products—from smartphones to laptops—are **100% authentic** and sourced directly from official manufacturers. Every item comes in its original, sealed retail packaging with the manufacturer's serial number.

2. What kind of warranty do you offer on gadgets?

Standard items come with a **1-year manufacturer warranty**. Additionally, we offer "Store Protection Plans" that cover accidental damage, such as cracked screens or liquid spills, for an additional 2 years.

3. How do I know if a component is compatible with my PC?

We provide detailed "Technical Specifications" on every product page. If you are building a custom PC, look for the **Socket Type** or **Form Factor** details. You can also use our Live Chat to speak with a technician before purchasing.

4. Can I return a laptop if I've already opened the box?

We accept returns within **14 days**. However, for electronics that have been opened or activated, a 15% restocking fee may apply. If the product is defective upon arrival, we will provide a **full replacement or refund** at no extra cost.

5. How do you ensure fragile items (like OLED TVs) are shipped safely?

We use **reinforced, double-walled boxing** and anti-static cushioning for all fragile components. High-value items (over $500) are shipped with full insurance and require a signature upon delivery for your peace of mind.
